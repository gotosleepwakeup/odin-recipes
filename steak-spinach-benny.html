Steak and Spinach Benny

Description

Tender steak and mushrooms adds an earthy flavor to this eggs benedict.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 English Muffin
  • 1/8C Cooked Spinach
  • 6 Strips of Cooked Steak
  • 1/2C Spinach
  • 1T Kewpie Mayonaise
  • Salt and pepper to season

Steps

  1. Cook that steak.
  2. Sautee that spinach.
  3. Sautee those mushrooms.
  4. Poach that egg.
  5. Toast that english muffin.
  6. Avengers assemble.
  7. Dole out the kewpie mayonaise.
  8. Sprinkle that salt and that pepper.
