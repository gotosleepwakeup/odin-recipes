BLT Benny

Description

Fresh tomatoes are a killer combo for this BLT Benny.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 English Muffin
  • 1T Green Onions
  • 1 Small Tomato
  • 1 Slice of Bacon
  • 1T Kewpie Mayonaise
  • Salt and pepper to season

Steps

  1. Cook that bacon.
  2. Poach that egg.
  3. Toast that english muffin.
  4. Slice that tomato.
  5. Avengers assemble.
  6. Dole out the kewpie mayonaise.
  7. Sprinkle those green onions.
  8. Sprinkle that salt and that pepper.
