Avocado Toast Benny

Description

Fresh avocado adds creaminess to this summer eggs benedict.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 English Muffin
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1T Kewpie Mayonaise
  • Salt and pepper to season

Steps

  1. Poach that egg.
  2. Mash that avocado
  3. Toast that english muffin.
  4. Avengers assemble.
  5. Dole out the kewpie mayonaise.
  6. Sprinkle that salt and that pepper.
