Salmon and Capers Benny

Description

Salmon and capers give this eggs benedict some pizazz.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 English Muffin
  • 1/4C Smoked Salmon
  • 2T Cream Cheese
  • 1T Capers
  • 1T Kewpie Mayonaise
  • Salt and pepper to season

Steps

  1. Poach that egg.
  2. Toast that english muffin.
  3. Spread that cream cheese.
  4. Avengers assemble.
  5. Dish out those capers.
  6. Dole out the kewpie mayonaise.
  7. Sprinkle that salt and that pepper.
